# 🌪️ Cyclone Management System

## 📌 Problem Statement

The main challenge during cyclones is not just predicting the storm, but effectively managing the situation on the ground.

Many coastal villages lack critical information such as:

* How risky their area is
* Which evacuation routes are safe
* Where shelters are located
* When help will arrive

This leads to unorganized evacuations and delayed relief efforts.

---

## 💡 Solution

We are building a **Smart Cyclone Management System** that helps authorities and communities make informed decisions before and during a cyclone.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. 📊 Risk Assessment using DICRA Dataset

* Uses national-level DICRA data including:

  * Climate risk
  * Population exposure
  * Vulnerability
  * Adaptive capacity
* Calculates a **Cyclone Risk Score**
* Classifies areas into:

  * High Risk
  * Medium Risk
  * Low Risk

---

### 2. 🔔 Early Warning Alerts

* Sends alerts to **high-risk villages**
* Encourages early evacuation preparation
* Reduces panic and last-minute chaos

---

### 3. 🗺️ Risk Visualization Map

* Displays a clear map showing risk levels across regions
* Helps authorities and citizens understand danger zones easily

---

### 4. 🚧 Safe Evacuation Route Planning

* Builds a road network connecting villages and shelters
* Detects blocked/flooded routes
* Suggests **alternate safe routes** in real time

---

### 5. 🏠 Shelter Capacity Monitoring

* Tracks occupancy of cyclone shelters
* Alerts when shelters are full
* Redirects people to nearby available shelters

---

### 6. 📦 Relief Resource Planning

* Optimizes distribution of:

  * Food kits
  * Rescue boats
  * Medical teams
* Prioritizes villages based on **risk level and population**

---

### 7. 🌊 Cyclone Intensity Simulation

* Interactive simulation showing:

  * Increasing cyclone intensity
  * Road blockages
  * Shelter demand
  * Relief requirements

---

## 🎯 Impact

This system goes beyond prediction and acts as a **complete disaster coordination platform**, enabling:

* ✅ Faster evacuation
* ✅ Better route planning
* ✅ Efficient shelter management
* ✅ Optimized relief distribution

---
